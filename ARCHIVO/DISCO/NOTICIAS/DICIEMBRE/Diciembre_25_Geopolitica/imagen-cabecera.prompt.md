# Prompt para Generación de Imagen: La Paz Armada

**Concepto**: Una ilustración editorial que visualiza la tesis "El Premio Nobel como infraestructura de guerra".

**Prompt sugerido (Midjourney / DALL-E 3)**:

> A conceptual editorial illustration divided into three analytical layers.
>
> **Center**: A large, golden Nobel Peace Prize medal. Half of the medal is pristine gold, the other half is rusting iron, transforming into a naval mine or a gear of war.
>
> **Layer 1 (Blue - Evidence)**: On the left, overlay of holographic legal documents and old wills, glowing in cyan blue, showing redacted text and contradictions.
>
> **Layer 2 (Red - Materiality)**: At the bottom, a thick, dark tide of crude oil rising, morphing into the silhouettes of modern warships enforcing a blockade. The oil reflects a crimson red light.
>
> **Layer 3 (Black - Power)**: In the background, a dark, shadowy network map connecting nodes labeled "Oslo", "DC", and "Caracas". Faint silhouettes of faceless bureaucrats in suits.
>
> **Atmosphere**: Serious, investigative journalism, "Dark Enlightenment" aesthetic. High contrast between the fake gold of the medal and the dark reality of the oil/war.
>
> **Style**: Digital collage, mixed media, political art, detailed, 8k resolution.

---

**Explicación de los elementos (Semiótica)**:

1.  **La Medalla/Mina**: La tesis central. La institución de paz (Nobel) esconde una función bélica.
2.  **El Petróleo/Barcos (Redflag)**: La base material del conflicto (Venezuela).
3.  **Los Documentos (Blueflag)**: La evidencia jurídica (la demanda de Assange).
4.  **La Red (Blackflag)**: La estructura de poder oculta.
